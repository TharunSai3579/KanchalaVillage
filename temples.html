SRI KADALIKESWARA SRIKADALIKESHAWARA BRAMARAMBIKA-DEVALAYAM

The Name kanchela was named after the lord shiva sri KADALIKESWARA swamy it is located on the east side of the village the road it is in was named as shivalayam Road. Foundation was laid on APRIL-13-2010. Every year the day was celebrated by doing rituals to lord kadalikeshwara and the main pooja was performed at sandhyasamayam(SUNSET time).

Danda Anjaneya Swamy

Danda Anjaneya swamy is Known as lord Hanuman which is located left to SRIKADALIKESHAWARA-BRAMARAMBIKA-DEVALAYAM. The lord Hanuman is powerful being a discipline of lord Sri Rama.On the occasion of HANUMAN JAYANTHI 108 Times the Hanuman chalisa is repeated. Everyone visits the temple mostly on Tuesday of the week.


Sri Varasiddi Vinayaka Swamy


Sri varasiddivinayaka swamy is located in kotha kanchela which comes after patha kanchela. The lord ganesh is popular for his powerful to the people of kanchela.The new temple is constructed on 21-April-2024.Every year A idol of lord ganesh is established and taken for procession.

Sri Veerla Ankalamma Thalli chennakesava swamy

SRI Veerla ankamma Thalli lakshmi chenna kesava swamy devalayam is constructed by the family"RAMIREDDY" as their family god and godess It has a history of 800 years.Every year the celebrations are performed for 3 days.
