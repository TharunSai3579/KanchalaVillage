kanchela


Kanchela is a small,serene village located in the rural hearthlands of state ANDHRA PRADESH of INDIA. The village is characterized by its picturesque landscape and lust greenery and fertile farmlands,located on the bank of Munneru the village is known for its agriculutral practices,with farming being the primary occupation. kanchela village comes under the Nandigama Mandal,NTR district of Andhra Pradesh.
The village is Divide into two parts PATHA kanchela and KOTHA kanchela.

AGRICULTURE

Most of the people in the village rely on Agriculture.kanchela is popular for its agricultural crops,vegetables and fruits including flowers The most produced crops are
  • paddy
  • sugarcane
  • maize
  • cotton
  • Chilli
and the leafy vegetables are
  • spinach
  • curry leaves
  • Kenaf Leaves
  • Amaranthus
  • malabar spinach
  • Coriander leaves
  • Mint Leaves
The seasonal fruits are MANGOES,it is famous for its flower of JASMINE. Jasmine is exported to all the nearby villages,towns and cities.NAGAMALLIPUVVU is the special flower in the village which it has a structure of shivalinga under a snake.




Animal Husbandry

people in the village feeds the animals Buffaloes,cows,sheep and goates and Birds like Hens. All the diary products milk,curd, ghee,cheese are homemade by themselves.




WATER RESOURCES

  • The Munneru is used as a drinking water it is tributary of River krishna.The water of it used for Drinking Purpose.
  • Borewell water is used for daily activities.
  • The water in the village tank is used to grow crops

HOW TO REACH

From Vijayawada side
If you are coming from Vijayawada side take a bus after crossing Keesara Munneru bridge after 100 meters you will get a road on your left. come along that road you will reach Kanchela

From Hyderabad side
Take a bus From Hyderabad and getdown at Nandigama and from there pickup a auto and getdown at kanchela road and head to oppsie side of the National Highway you will head a road on your left come along that road you will reach kanchela
